What is DSC and how does it work?

A digital certificate is an electronic document issued by (CA) and all the CA's are controlled by CCA's.
It contains the public key for a Digital Signature and specifies the identity associated with the key, such as the name of an organization or individual.
The certificate is used to confirm that the public key belongs to a specific organization or individual.

How to login for Aadhar based approvals?

There are two levels of Approvals:

  • Level: 1
  • Level: 2


  • Step 1: Click on the ERA server URL → Queue → eKYC queue → Level 1 or Level 2 → Select date → Select eKYC type(Aadhar based) → View
  • Step 2: Select the record
  • Step 3: Click on Approve/Reject Selected
  • Step 4: Applicants details will appear on the screen
  • Step 5: Check the below details to approve:
    • Verify name of the applicant - Special cases: Sometimes applicant will not be able to speak (dum), in that scenario you can approve the record
    • Verify Photo of the Applicant
    • Verify the random code
    • Applicant must speak their name and random code in the video

Important Note

  • Applicant must say their name in the video
  • Passport size photo must be attached
  • Applicant must say correct Random Code in the video
  • Only the applicant must be present/speak in the video
  • Video will be recorded for 35 seconds with the applicant present in it
  • Check name of the applicant
  • Check the random code
  • If you are unable to understand the language, ignore the record or get help from team lead
  • Voice of the applicant must match the lip sync of the applicant
  • If audio or video is not clear, please reject the record


Step 6: After verifying all details, click on Approve or Reject



Note: The above steps are applicable for both Level 1 and Level 2 Aadhaar based approval

How to login for PAN based approvals?

There are two levels of Approvals:

  • Level: 1
  • Level: 2

  • Step 1: Click on ERA server URL → Queue → eKYC queue → Level 1 or Level 2 → Select date → Select eKYC type(Pan based) → View
  • Step 2: Select the record
  • Step 3: Click on Approve/Reject Selected
  • Step 4: Details of the selected applicant will appear on the screen
  • Step 5: Check below details to approve:
    • Check ID proofs such as Aadhar, Pan or any government ID
    • Verify name, DOB, Pan number with Pan Card
    • Applicant should show both original proof in the video
    • Verify Random Code in the video
    • Old PAN card is acceptable

Important Note

  • Applicant must say their name in the video
  • Passport size photo must be attached
  • Applicant must say correct Random Code in the video
  • Only the applicant must be present/speak in the video
  • Video will be recorded for 35 seconds with the applicant present in it
  • Check name of the applicant
  • Check the random code
  • If you are unable to understand the language, ignore the record or get help from team lead
  • Voice of the applicant must match the lip sync of the applicant
  • If audio or video is not clear, please reject the record
  • Photocopy(Xerox) of the uploaded attachments is also acceptable
  • Both original proofs should be shown in video
  • Applicant must show their complete face in the video


Acceptable Proofs

Documents as proof of address(Any one)
  • Aadhaar (eKYC Service)
  • Telephone bill
  • Electricity bill
  • Water bill
  • Gas connection
  • Bank statements signed by the bank
  • Service Tax/VAT/Sales tax registration certificate
  • Driving licence/Registration Certificate
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Property Tax/Coroporation/Municipal corporation receipt
  • Any government issued ID with photo, name, and address


Documents as proof of identity(Any one)
  • Aadhaar (eKYC Service)
  • Passport
  • Driving licence
  • PAN card
  • Post Office ID
  • Bank Account Passbook/Statement containing photo and signed by the individual with attestation by the concerned Bank official.
  • Photo ID card issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs of Centre/State Government
  • Any government issued ID with photo, name, and address


With above documents the following conditions will apply:
  • 1. Validity of the Address Proof: In case of any utility bills like electricity, water, gas, and telephone bill in the name of the applicant. Recent proof, but not earlier than 3 months from the date of application shall be attached.
  • 2. Identity & Address proof must be attached in a single document instead of two seperate documents; however, if the address in the Photo-ID is different from the address given in the application, then a separate address proof shall be insisted for
  • 3. Digitally signed documents: Digitally signed photo ID document by the issuer can be accepted in electronic format where CA can validate the Digital Signature. In such case, CA shall double check the photo with the video. The document shall be preserved along with its password(if any) for future references(applicable for ePAN, Driving License, etc being issued by respective issuers in digitally signed form)
Note: The above steps are applicable for both Level 1 and Level 2 PAN based approval

  • Level: 2

  • Step 3: Click on Approve/Reject Selected
  • Step 4: Details of the selected applicant will appear on the screen
  • Step 5: Check below details to approve:
    • Verify Name, DOB, PAN number, and Pan card
    • Employee ID or authorization of the applicant by organization is acceptable
    • Verify name of the applicant and Random Code
    • Applicant must show uploaded documents in the video
    • Original documents are only acceptable in the video


Note: In organizational based and Government based, the video will be recorded for 25 seconds. If the applicant shows only one ID proof, then please approve it after verifying the details
If the video is for 30 Seconds and if the applicant shows only one proof, then please reject it even though the details are correct, because in 30 seconds the applicant must show both ID proofs

  • Applicant must say correct Random Code in the video

  • Video will be recorded for 35 seconds with the applicant present in it
  • Check name of the applicant
  • Check the random code
  • If you are unable to understand the language, ignore the record or get help from team lead
  • Voice of the applicant must match the lip sync of the applicant
  • If audio or video is not clear, please reject the record
  • PAN card is mandatory in organizational video
