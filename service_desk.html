Service Desk

Introduction: -
  • Sify service desk is a platform for customer service management.
  • A service desk is a communications center that provides a single point of contact (SPOC) between a company and its customers, employees and business partners.
  • The purpose of a service desk is to ensure that users receive appropriate help in a timely manner.
Types of customers support: -
  • Service desk providing two types of customer support.
  • DC COLO ( Data Center Co-location) - We provide only space to the Customer.
  • DC IT ( Data Center Information Technology) - We provide both space and IT services.
DC COLO: -
  • DC related support belongs to person allow to the data center and material, movements in/out.
  • Cage/Rack access and maintenance.
  • Activity related etc.
  • For ex: - Ups and floor maintenance activity
  • Sify can provide the space for mounting the devices.
