Implementation of New Link:
  • Sales Team will receive a request for New Link Installation.
  • Sales Head will check the Companies authorization and approve the request to proceed further.
  • After the approval of Sales Head, it will assign to an PM - Project Manager to check the feasibility and PM will take the responsibility to complete the project on time.
  • Internal team will create OSC Opportunity ID to customer to find customer complete details at one point.
  • Customer must agree with company norms and need to sign on the agreement with companies authorized stamp.

FEASIBILITY:
  • One of the engineers from Sify will visit the Location to check the availability of our service at Customer Site.
    • IF YES:
  • Sify Team will take charge to proceed further.
    • IF NO:
  • Check with regional team who will be available at nearby location like (Tata, Airtel, BSNL, Bharathi, TCL, etc)
  • After the completion of Feasibility, Project Manager will release a PO – Purchase Order which includes complete cost and infrastructure needed for the New connection at particular location.
  • After receiving PO from customer, a special RDSE – Regional Service Delivery Engineer will take handover for installation of required hardware to customer location.
  • Commercials and Hardware parts are released from nearby stores to customer location.
  • RDSE or Sify Field Team will Install the Hardware parts at Customer Site.
  • Internal Team will generate a unique Link ID and it will be added into IONI and AAKAASH Portals.
  • After completion of On boarding, link will be handed over to Service Operations Team for Maintenance.


Workflow:
  • Sales Team Head Approval
  • Assign to Project Manager
  • Creating OSC Opportunity ID
  • Agreement with Customer
  • Feasibility Check
  • Releasing Purchase Order
  • Shipping Hardware material to Site
  • Installation of Hardware
  • Link Onboarding
  • Handover to Service Operations

Temporary Disconnection:
  • If customer failed to pay the bills on time, the link will be automatically disconnected
  • Process 1 - Fusion Collection Team Initiate > Service Now TD (Success) > Temp Disconnected


Temporary Re-Connection:
  • If customer proceeds to pay the bills after temporary disconnection, the link will be reconnected and it will take 24 hours to reflect after receiving payment
  • Process 1 - Fusion Collection Team Initiate > Service Now TD (Success) > Re-connected
  • Process 2- Fusion Collection Team Initiate > Service Now TD (Fail) > CPE Team > Re-connected


Permanent Disconnection:
  • If customer requested for Permanent Disconnection, need to forward the request to sify.creditcontrol@sifycorp.com
  • Process 1 -Oracle (Collection Team Raise Request) > Service Now PD (Success) > Fusion Asset Recovery Team (WSG) > SOT Team > Disconnected
  • Oracle (Collection Team Raise Request) > Service Now PD (Fail) > Fusion CPE Initiate PD > SOT > Commercial Team (Other Lastmile) > Disconnected
  • Process 2 - TD Links (After 60days) > Service Now PD (Success) > Fusion Asset Recovery Team (WSG) > SOT Team > Disconnected
  • TD Links (After 60days) > Service Now PD (Success) > Fusion Asset Recovery Team (WSG) > SOT Team > Disconnected


Workflow:
  • Disconnection Request Raised
  • Ticket raised on ASSET Recovery
  • Will recover the assets from customer location
  • Collecting all pending request
  • Permanently Disconnected
